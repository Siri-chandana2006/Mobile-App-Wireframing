# User Research

## Research Goal

To understand the difficulties students face while finding and sharing study materials.

## Findings

* Students often receive notes through multiple WhatsApp groups.
* Finding old notes is difficult.
* Students prefer a centralized platform.
* Subject-wise organization is important.

## User Needs

* Upload notes
* Download notes
* Search notes
* Simple interface

## Conclusion

Students need a single platform to access academic resources efficiently.
